Do you want to make Kitco your home page?
    In Firefox:
  1. In the TOOLS menu, select OPTIONS.
  2. At the top of the dialog box, select the GENERAL tab.
  3. In the HOME PAGE text box, type www.kitco.com then click OK.
  1. Press CTRL-F12.
  2. Click the General tab.
  3. Type www.kitco.com in the field for homepage.
  4. Click Ok.
    In Chrome:
  1. Click the settings icon in the top right corner of your browser. Select Settings.
  2. Under On startup select Open a specific page or set of pages. Click Set pages. Enter www.kitco.com, and click OK.
  3. Under Appearance click Show home button. Click Change, enter www.kitco.com, and click OK.
  1. Pull down the Safari menu.
  2. Select Preferences.
  3. In the General tab's homepage entry box, type www.kitco.com and close the Preferences window.
